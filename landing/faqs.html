General Questions

What is Shule-Net?

Shule-Net is a cloud-based school management system that helps schools manage students, teachers, fees, and academic performance in one platform.

Who can use Shule-Net?

Administrators, teachers, students, and even parents can interact with the system depending on assigned roles.

System Features

Can I manage multiple schools?

Yes, Shule-Net supports multi-school (multi-tenant) management from a single platform.

Does it support fee tracking?

Yes, you can track payments, generate invoices, and manage financial reports easily.

Is it mobile friendly?

Absolutely. Shule-Net is fully responsive and works across phones, tablets, and desktops.

Security & Access

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use secure authentication, encrypted storage, and regular backups to protect your data.

Can students access their results?

Yes, students have their own dashboard where they can view academic progress, results, and fee status.
